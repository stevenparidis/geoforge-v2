Diagram: Diagrams of sedimentary primary structures showing horizontal bedding, cross-bedding, and graded bedding; strike-and-dip notation on tilted/dipping beds; block diagrams of horizontal vs dipping strata in outcrop.

Description: Practical structural geology introduction covering horizontal and dipping sedimentary layers. Explains strike and dip measurement as the primary descriptor of a dipping bed's orientation.

Useful for auditing: Provides the reference geometry for GeoForge's horizontal and dipping layer classes. Strike-and-dip notation diagrams help verify that GeoForge correctly represents the angle and direction of dip in its cross-section view.
