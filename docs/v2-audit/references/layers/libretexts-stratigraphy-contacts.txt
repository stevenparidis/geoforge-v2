Description: Sedimentary geology stratigraphy chapter reviewing conformable contacts and unconformable contacts. Provides cross-sections showing how multi-layer sequences look in outcrop with parallel conformable bedding vs eroded/tilted surfaces.

Useful for auditing: The conformable multi-layer cross-sections are the baseline reference for GeoForge's "layers" feature class. Correct rendering requires parallel bedding planes, consistent layer thickness laterally, and sharp contacts between layers.
