Description: Comprehensive illustrated reference covering all six GeoForge fault subtypes. Listric fault section includes two dedicated cross-sections, one showing the characteristic concave-upward geometry flattening to a basal detachment, and a second showing a rollover anticline in the hanging wall.

Useful for auditing: The listric and oblique-slip diagrams are the most important reference for GeoForge subtypes not well-covered by textbook sources. The rollover-anticline geometry produced by listric faults is an advanced visual feature to verify. Strike-slip plan-view schematic validates GeoForge's map-view rendering of horizontal block offset.
